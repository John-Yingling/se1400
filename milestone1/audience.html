The Audience:

The Elders Scrolls series has been a long on going series since the first games release in 1994 under the title of The Elder Scrolls: Arena. The most recent game in the series was The Elder Scrolls Online and has sold around 20 million copies and has 5 million monthly players. I'm not entirely sure where the elder scrolls player base is, but if I had to guess it would have to mainly be played by Americans as the the company who developed the games, Bethesda Softworks, is an American company. Seeing as how the game series has been out for 30+ years the age demographic varies. While I can't find anything specifically on the Elder Scrolls, the gender demographic for the elder scrolls is most likely a little more male than female when looking at video game gender statisitcs.
