The Elder Scrolls Info

The Elder Scrolls is an open-world rpg game series developed by Bethesda Softworks. The games have been super successful with The Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind (2002), The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion (2006), and The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim each winning game of the year awards from multiple different parties. The series has sold millions of copies. The first game released in 1994 and has had five main games and several spin-off games. As of April 22nd, 2025, The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion Remastered released with fans ecstaic and many fans saying the nostlgia is amazing. The Elder Scrolls VI was announced in 2018, but will not be released until 2026 or possibly later.
